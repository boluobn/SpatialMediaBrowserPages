About YJing

YJing is your central hub for managing and exploring personal spatial photos and videos on visionOS. Unlike other apps that offer curated content, this application focuses on your memories, allowing you to organize, store, and relive these memories in a whole new dimension.

Key Features

Local Media Management

Store and organize your spatial media files locally on your device.

Import Options

Import media from the Photos app or upload from Mac to keep your collection up to date.

Directory Management

Create and manage multi-level directories to efficiently categorize your media.

Favorites Management

Mark your favorite photos and videos for quick access.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What file types does YJing support?

Supports all file types available in the Photos app, including spatial photos and videos.

2. How do I import media files from Mac?

You can upload media files to the app's Documents directory. Please refer to the MAC user manual's file synchronization instructions for detailed steps.

3. Does the app support cloud storage?

Currently, YJing focuses on local storage and does not support cloud storage functionality.
