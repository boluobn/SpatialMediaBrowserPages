Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how the application developer ("we", "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use Spatial Media Browser (the "Application").

Information We Collect

This application accesses your device's photo library to display and manage photos and spatial videos. This may include access to related metadata (such as location data), but we do not collect or transmit your photos, videos, or metadata to external servers.

  • Local Storage: The application stores some preferences on your device, such as favorited photos and videos. This data is stored locally only and is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • No Usage Data Collection: This application does not collect analytics data.

How We Use Information

  • Photo Library Data: Used to provide functionality for browsing and managing photos and spatial videos.
  • Preferences: Used to remember your favorites and settings for future access.

Data Security

Since data is only stored locally on your device, you are responsible for your device's security. Please ensure appropriate device settings (such as password protection) to prevent unauthorized access.

No Third-Party Sharing

This application does not share your data with any third parties, including photos, videos, metadata, or preferences.

User Choices

You can manage photo library permissions through your device settings to control application data access.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Please review it periodically to stay informed of the latest version.
